About

This demo is available for download, with all the media, here . Feel free to download and experiment with it using Belle's editor.

To just run the demo, you don't need the editor. You can just download and extract the the archive above to anywhere on your computer and double-click the index.html .

If you want to tinker with it, do the following:

  1. Download Belle and install it.
  2. Download and extract the archive mentioned above to anywhere in your computer.
  3. Open Belle, go to File > Open Project, go to the directory you extracted before and open the file game_data.js .
